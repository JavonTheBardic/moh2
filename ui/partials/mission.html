Our Mission

Melody of Hope will seek to use music as a means to raise funds and spread awareness for various non-profits located around our community.

“Let the music bring hope”

Goal

The goal of the partnerships is to help provide and raise funds while becoming a voice and advocate for the causes. Together we can target multiple issues that plagues our local community and build a network of supporters. Together we can target multiple issues that plague our local communities, raise up a network of supporters, and contribute to the sustainable growth of our nation and further serve tomorrows generation.

Target Non-Profits
-Children at Risk
-North Texas Food Bank
-Make-A-Wish
-The Net
-Bully Suicide Project
-Snowball Express
-Fine Arts Chamber Players

Goals
-Promote public awareness
-Raise Funds
-Recruit, train, and support volunteers
-Support local artist
-Create
-Support the arts
-Provide an enjoyable community
-Network with other non-profits
-Change the music industry

Purpose

The purpose of the partnership is to help decrease the dependency syndrome, promote productivity, and ultimately establish healthy communities. Fundraising is the hardest aspect to sustaining a non profit, Melody of Hope was created to help raise that funding. Melody of hope will also help to encourage our communities to grow into a self-supporting sustainable manner for generations to come.
